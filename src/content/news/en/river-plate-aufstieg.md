What a day for FC Treptow: our second team, River Plate, have secured promotion. After a strong second half of the season, it was confirmed on the final matchday that we're moving up.

Thank you to every player, coach and fan who backed the lads all season long. The celebrations happened right at the Willi-Sänger sports ground, deep into the night.

Next season we take on a higher league. If you want to be part of it: trial training is open to everyone.
