The Golden Ticket enters its next round. Every ticket directly supports club work, from new training gear to pitch maintenance.

Tickets are available at every home game at the club office and via kontakt@fc-treptow.de. The draw takes place at the next home matchday, and winners will be announced here on the website.

Thank you to everyone supporting FC Treptow.
